About geoMaster

geoMaster is a time-based geography quiz game featuring four modes that test your knowledge of countries, flags, capitals, and facts.

Compete against the clock to achieve the lowest completion time. Each session contains 7 questions. Incorrect answers instantly add a 60-second penalty to your time.
